Hi! I’m
Joas A Santos
Cyber Security Professional

Joas Antonio dos Santos is a seasoned Head of Cybersecurity and Engineer, specializing in offensive security, DevSecOps, threat intelligence, incident response, malware analysis, and cloud security. He has authored influential books like "Introduction to Offensive Security: An Approach for Pentesters and Red Teams," and "Introduction to Red Team Operations: A Basic Guide for Your Red Team Operations, Volumes 1 and 2," with articles featured in HVCK, Hakin9, PenTest Magazine, and eForensic Magazine. An active cybersecurity community contributor, Joas has produced thousands of content pieces on LinkedIn and GitHub, published over 20 CVEs, Subject Matter Expert in Hack The Box, Ambassador in APISec University, Post Graduate Teacher in Vincit University and is known for his research in offensive security techniques such as PhantomsGate. He has spoken at major conferences and institutions, including RoadSec, Mind The Sec, H2HC, Red Team Village, White Hat Event Latam, FATEC Americana, Vincit, and Womcy, and is currently dedicated to research and writing, aiming for a PhD.
